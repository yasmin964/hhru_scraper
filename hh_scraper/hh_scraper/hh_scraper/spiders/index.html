About This Analysis

This dashboard presents insights derived from analysis of 1,580 sales manager job postings across multiple industries. The data was collected from major job platforms and processed using natural language processing techniques to extract and categorize professional skills.

Visualizations are interactive - hover over elements to see detailed information, click and drag to explore different perspectives, and use the toolbar in the top-right corner of each chart for additional options.
